# Social Media Playbook & Content Strategy

## 🎯 Mission Statement
To show the world that **anyone** can create high-quality games without knowing how to code. The barrier to entry is gone—now it's about vision + prompts.

## 👥 Target Audience
- Aspiring game developers (especially those intimidated by coding).
- Indie Hackers / Solopreneurs.
- Gamers interested in Roguelikes/Sci-Fi.
- Tech enthusiasts curious about AI-assisted development (Antigravity).

## 🗣️ Brand Voice & Tone
- **Genuinely Human:** Talk like a real person, not a marketing bot. Admit when things are hard or when a feature surprised you.
- **Indie Perspective:** Speak from the trenches. It's about being a solo creator figuring it out, not a "tech guru."
- **Raw & Real:** Build connection through honesty. "Building in Public" means showing the messy parts, not just the polished AI results.
- **Direct & Relatable:** No "smarty ass" talk. Use plain language that invites people into the journey rather than preaching to them.
- **Vision-Driven:** Focus on the dream of the game, not just the prompts used to get there.

## 🔑 Key Pillars & Content Themes
1.  **No-Code Triumphs:** Showcasing complex features built purely with prompts (e.g., Inventory, Hex Grid, Boss AI).
2.  **Before vs. After:** Visual progress comparisons (Day 1 vs. Day 30).
3.  **Behind the Scenes:** Screenshots of the "Module Matrix," "Skill Trees," or specific mechanics.
4.  **Community Building:** Asking for feedback on art, mechanics, or names.

## 🛠️ Media Creation Toolkit
To keep the "Raw & Real" vibe, use these tools for quick, high-quality captures:

### 1. The "Dev-Log" Special
- **Tool:** [ScreenToGif](https://www.screentogif.com/)
- **Use for:** Quick 5-10 second loops of UI interactions (like the Hex Grid) or bug reports. Great for showing "Before/After" comparisons.

### 2. High-Fidelity Gameplay
- **Tool:** [OBS Studio](https://obsproject.com/)
- **Use for:** Recording 30-60 seconds of high-intensity combat (Radiation Core, Terror Pulse). Lower the bitrate for small file sizes that play instantly on Twitter.

### 3. Social Media Editing (Captions & Zooms)
- **Tool:** [CapCut](https://www.capcut.com/)
- **Use for:** Adding the "human" touch. Zoom in on a specific logic block or a UI element. Add auto-captions for your "Genuinely Human" voiceovers.

### 4. Direct Screenshots
- **Tool:** Windows + Shift + S (Snipping Tool)
- **Use for:** Static shots of the "Blueprint Archive" or "Leaderboards."
