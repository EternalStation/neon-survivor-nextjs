# Boss Encyclopedia

A comprehensive intelligence archive detailing the tiered evolution, primary designations, and specific skill protocols of all identified Boss-tier entities within the Neon Survivor theater.

## Global Protocols

All bosses utilize the [Boss Stages](docs/Mechanics/Core/BossStages.md) protocol, transitioning through three stages at 66% and 33% HP.

---

## Shape Designations

### Circle Boss: The Kinetic Predator
Specializes in high-mobility displacement and area suppression.

- **Level 2 - Dash Strike**: Periodically locks onto the player's vector and performs a high-velocity dash.
- **Level 3 - Gravity Cyclone**: Generates a gravitational vortex that pulls the player directly toward the boss's center.
- **Level 4 - Soul Suppression**: Channels a devastating soul-drain field that reduces player collection efficiency and grants the boss temporary invulnerability.

### Square Boss: The Fortress Guardian
A defensive monolith that utilizes shields and reflective protocols.

- **Level 2 - Kinetic Thorns**: Reflects a percentage of contact damage back to the player.
- **Level 3 - Orbital Aegis**: Deploys three rotating orbital shields. The boss is completely invulnerable while any shield remains active. Shields periodically regenerate.
- **Level 4 - Piercing Thorns**: Contact damage reflection now ignores player armor values.

### Triangle Boss: The Berserk Reaver
Agile and aggressive, focusing on speed and offensive denial.

- **Level 2 - Berserk Overdrive**: Periodically enters a hyper-active state with vastly increased movement speed and erratic movement patterns.
- **Level 3 - Projectile Deflection**: While in Berserk state, the boss generates a deflection field that nullifies or reflects incoming projectiles.
- **Level 4 - Bio-Nullification**: emits a persistent field that completely disables all player healing and lifesteal protocols while the boss is alive.

### Diamond Boss: The Orbital Artillery
Long-range precision specialist utilizing directed energy weapons.

- **Level 2 - Focused Beam**: Charges and fires a concentrated solar laser at the player's last known position.
- **Level 3 - Satellite Strike**: Coordinates with orbital platforms to rain multiple high-damage energy beams across a widespread area.
- **Level 4 - Binary Beam**: Fires dual parallel beams that gradually narrow their focus, creating an escaping window that shrinks over time.
- **Level 5 - Crystal Lattice**: Traps the player within a pentagonal laser fence, restricting movement and dealing heavy damage upon contact with the field.

### Pentagon Boss: The Phalanx Commander
Tactical strategist that utilizes minions and symbiotic links.

- **Level 1 - Soul Link**: Passively links its vitality to nearby regular enemies, distributing damage taken among all connected entities.
- **Level 2 - Rocket Swarm**: Launches a semi-guided barrage of drone rockets in a wide spread.
- **Level 3 - Parasitic Link**: Establishes a direct life-line with the player at close range, draining player HP to regenerate the boss's core.
- **Level 4 - Phalanx Rush**: Commands a coordinated line of 8 drones to form a wall and charge in unison across the arena.

### Abomination Boss: The Anomaly Overlord
The pinnacle of evolutionary corruption, appearing as a massive amorphous threat.

- **Passives - Overlord Burn**: Passively generates an intense thermal field (Inferno) that deals damage proportional to the player's proximity and the boss's current Stage.
- **Stage 2 - Expanding Inferno**: The radius of the thermal field increases significantly.
- **Stage 3 - Eternal Flame**: The boss begins regenerating health while the player is within its inferno radius.
