# Diamond Boss

The Diamond Boss is a high-tier mechanical adversary characterized by its geometric precision and area-denial energy attacks.

## Core Mechanics

### Phase 1: Basic Pulse (Tier 1)
The boss maintains a safe distance from the player, pulsing with energy and attempting to stay within tactical range.

### Phase 2: Focused Beam (Tier 2)
- **Telegraph**: Charges energy orbs around its core, projecting a tracking line towards the player.
- **Attack**: Fires a high-intensity laser beam after a brief delay.
- **Interaction**: This beam instantly destroys any nearby Zombies (active minions).

### Phase 3: Satellite Strikes (Tier 3)
- **Telegraph**: Spawns multiple targeting reticles on the ground near the player.
- **Attack**: Calls down orbital beams from above, dealing high damage in circular zones.
- **Visuals**: Strike colors shift based on the current Era palette.

### Phase 4: Chrono-V Beam (Tier 4)
- **Attack**: Instead of a single beam, the boss fires two lasers in a wide V-shape that slowly narrows over time.
- **Pressure**: Forces the player to stay within a shrinking safe zone or escape before the beams converge.

### Phase 5: Crystal Electronic Fence (Tier 5)
- **Telegraph**: Spawns 5 energy crystals in a pentagonal formation centered on the player's last position.
- **Attack**: Crystals connect via high-voltage electric arcs, forming a pentagonal fence that traps the player inside.
- **Damage**: Touching the arcs deals rapid damage based on a percentage of the player's health.
- **Telegraphing**: A dash sound signalizes the end of the ability when the crystals vanish.
