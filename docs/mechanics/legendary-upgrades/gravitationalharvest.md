# Gravitational Harvest (Fusion)

**Fusion Type:** Singularity Harvest
**Ingredients:** Essence Syphon (EcoHP) + Epicenter (DefEpi)

## Core Mechanics

### Eternal Pull
For every enemy killed while they are trapped inside the Epicenter zone, the current duration of the Epicenter and the player's Gravitational Harvest active ability is extended by **0.1 seconds**. This allows for significantly longer-lasting zones when facing dense hordes.

### Trapped Resilience
While an Epicenter is active, **10% of the total damage** taken by the player from any source is instantly reflected back to **all enemies** currently trapped within the Epicenter's gravity well. This provides both defensive utility and powerful offensive feedback during high-pressure situations.

## Integrated Perks

### Essence Syphon (Enhanced)
Inherits all perks from Essence Syphon with specialized scaling:
- **LVL 1:** +0.2 Max HP per kill
- **LVL 2:** +0.06 HP/sec per kill
- **LVL 3:** +0.2% Max HP per kill
- **LVL 4:** +0.06% HP/sec per kill

### Epicenter
Inherits all perks from Epicenter:
- **LVL 1:** Deals 25% of player's DMG every second and slows enemies by 50%.
- **LVL 2:** 2% Pull strength towards the center.
- **LVL 3:** 20% Radius growth over 10s.
- **LVL 4:** Executes enemies below 5% MAX HP threshold.
