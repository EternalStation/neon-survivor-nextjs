# Boss Scaling Update

## Overview
Normal bosses have been significantly buffed to maintain challenge throughout the run, especially in the 8-15 minute mid-game window where they previously felt too weak.

## Changes
- **Base HP Multiplier**: Increased from **25x** to **30x**.
- **Progressive Scaling**: Moved from a simple linear progression to a tiered aggressive interval system. 
  - Every 5 minutes, the rate at which boss HP grows per minute increases.
  
### Scaling Intervals
| Time Range | Previous Growth Rate | New Growth Rate |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 0–5 Minutes | +1 HP Mult / Min | **+2 HP Mult / Min** |
| 5–10 Minutes | +2 HP Mult / Min | **+5 HP Mult / Min** |
| 10–15 Minutes | +3 HP Mult / Min | **+10 HP Mult / Min** |
| 15–20 Minutes | +4 HP Mult / Min | **+15 HP Mult / Min** |
| 20+ Minutes | +5 HP Mult / Min | **+20 HP Mult / Min** |
