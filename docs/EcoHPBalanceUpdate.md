# EcoHP Balance Update - March 2026

This document describes the balance changes made to the **Essence Syphon (EcoHP)** legendary upgrade.

## Changes Overview

The scaling values for Essence Syphon have been reduced to bring its late-game power more in line with other economic and defensive options.

| Level | Feature | Previous Value | New Value | Change |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **LVL 1** | Max HP per kill (Flat) | +0.1 | **+0.05** | -50% |
| **LVL 2** | HP Regen per kill (Flat) | +0.03 | **+0.02** | -33% |
| **LVL 3** | Max HP % per kill | +0.1% | **+0.05%** | -50% |
| **LVL 4** | HP Regen % per kill | +0.03% | **+0.02%** | -33% |

## Impact on Fusions

Fusions that utilize EcoHP (**Neutron Star** and **Gravitational Harvest**) use a `2.0x` modifier on these base values in their enhanced versions. Consequently, their scaling has also been adjusted:

- **LVL 1 Enhanced HP:** +0.1 per kill (was +0.2)
- **LVL 2 Enhanced Regen:** +0.04 per kill (was +0.06)
- **LVL 3 Enhanced HP %:** +0.1% per kill (was +0.2%)
- **LVL 4 Enhanced Regen %:** +0.04% per kill (was +0.06%)

## Rationale

Essence Syphon was providing excessive health pools in long sessions, often making the player virtually unkillable without specific boss mechanics. These adjustments preserve the "infinite scaling" identity while slowing down the total growth rate.
