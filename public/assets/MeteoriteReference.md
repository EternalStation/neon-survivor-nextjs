# Meteorite Rarity Levels - Visual Guide & Prompts

This document serves as a reference for the visual style and prompts used for the meteorite-like crystals. All images follow a **retro pixel art style** (128x128) with sharp pixels, irregular jagged shapes, and transparent backgrounds.

## Active Rarities (1-6)

The game uses 6 main rarities: **Anomalous, Radiant, Void, Eternal, Divine, Singularity**.

Each rarity has 3 Quality States: **Broken**, **Damaged**, **New**.
Additionally, any meteorite can have the **Corrupted** status, which enhances its perks.

---

## 1. Anomalous (M0) - Cyan / Glitchy
- **Visual**: Faintly translucent teal/cyan crystals with flickering digital artifacts. Small jagged rock fragments floating in a stable glitch field.
- **Prompt**: `128x128 pixel art of a cosmic meteorite rock, 'Anomalous' rarity. Faintly translucent teal/cyan crystals with flickering digital artifacts. Small jagged rock fragments floating in a stable glitch field. Intense cyan neon rim glow on edges only. STRICTLY no outer effects, no sparks, no particles outside silhouette. Retro style, sharp pixels, transparent background. Centered. Label 'Anomalous' in small pixel text at the bottom.`

## 2. Radiant (M1) - Gold
- **Visual**: Irregular jagged rock shell in dull gray-gold. Intense golden-white radiant energy core pulsing strongly and VISIBLY TRAPPED inside, bursting against the rock shell from within. Larger cracks and craters revealing MORE of the bright inner light.
- **Prompt**: `128x128 pixel art of a cosmic meteorite rock, 'Radiant' rarity. irregular jagged rock shell in dull gray-gold. Intense golden-white radiant energy core pulsing strongly and VISIBLY TRAPPED inside, bursting against the rock shell from within. Larger cracks and craters revealing MORE of the bright inner light. Energy feels pressurized and contained but ready to burst. Intense yellow-gold neon rim glow on edges only. STRICTLY no outer effects, no sparks, no particles outside silhouette. Retro style, sharp pixels, transparent background. Centered. Label 'Radiant' in small pixel text at the bottom.`

## 3. Void (M2) - Dark Red (formerly Abyss)
- **Visual**: Dangerous dark red core with angry red rim, chaotic fire-like particles trapped inside. Very thin rock shell in dark gray-red.
- **Prompt**: `128x128 pixel art of a cosmic meteorite rock, 'Void' rarity. Irregular jagged shape, very thin dark gray-red rock shell. Dangerous dark red energy core angrily glowing and visible through deep cracks (chaotic fire-like patterns trapped within). Intense crimson-red neon rim glow on edges only. STRICTLY no outer effects, no sparks, no particles outside silhouette. Retro style, sharp pixels, transparent background. Centered. Label 'Void' in small pixel text at the bottom.`

## 4. Eternal (M3) - Dark Gold / Metallic
- **Visual**: Ancient polished gold with metallic sheen, intricate golden energy veins. Rock shell should feel metallic but still like an irregular asteroid.
- **Prompt**: `128x128 pixel art of a cosmic meteorite rock, 'Eternal' rarity. Irregular jagged shape, ancient polished gold rock shell with metallic sheen. Intricate golden energy veins pulsing through surface cracks. Intense golden neon rim glow. STRICTLY no outer effects, no sparks, no particles outside silhouette. Retro style, sharp pixels, transparent background. Centered. Label 'Eternal' in small pixel text at the bottom.`

## 5. Divine (M4) - White / Pearlescent
- **Visual**: Pure white crystal-infused rock with soft ice-blue halo, rainbow diffraction edges visible through shell.
- **Prompt**: `128x128 pixel art of a cosmic meteorite rock, 'Divine' rarity. Irregular jagged shape, pearlescent white rock shell with soft ice-blue halo. Rainbow diffraction edges visible through deep crystalline cracks. Holy light beams trapped inside. Intense ice-blue neon rim glow. STRICTLY no outer effects, no sparks, no particles outside silhouette. Retro style, sharp pixels, transparent background. Centered. Label 'Divine' in small pixel text at the bottom.`

## 6. Singularity (M5) - Magenta / Black Void
- **Visual**: Near-black void center with crushing indigo-violet accretion disk effect trapped inside shell. Swirling black hole particles.
- **Prompt**: `128x128 pixel art of a cosmic meteorite rock, 'Singularity' rarity. Irregular jagged shape, black obsidian-like rock shell. Crushing indigo-violet accretion disk effect visible through central voids. Swirling black hole particles trapped inside. Extreme indigo-violet neon rim glow. Most epic and powerful. STRICTLY no outer effects, no sparks, no particles outside silhouette. Retro style, sharp pixels, transparent background. Centered. Label 'Singularity' in small pixel text at the bottom.`
