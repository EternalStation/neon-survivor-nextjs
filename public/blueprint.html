Sector Analysis

The Nexus is divided into three critical functional zones. Players must balance time between Economic harvesting and Combat clearing to maintain the Defense integrity of the core.

  • Economic: High resource density, low enemy threat.
  • Combat: Max XP gain, Elite spawns only.
  • Defense: Static turret nodes, hazard management.
