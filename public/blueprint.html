Global Modifiers
Economic Stability
Kill XP scales by 40 + (2 * Level). Spawn rate starts at 1.6/s and increases by 0.1 every 60 seconds.

Portal Logic

Manual Activation

Portals no longer cycle automatically. Players can open all connecting portals at any time by paying 5 Meteorite Dust. Once opened, they remain active for 10s before destabilizing.

Leveling & Scaling

  • Experience Requirement: Starts at 250 XP. Each level increases the required XP by 10% (compounding).
  • XP Scaling Formula: 250 * (1.10 ^ (Level - 1))
  • Enemy HP Growth: 80 * (1.186^m) * (1 + (m * log2(2 + m) / 30)) → 20k @ 20min, 250M @ 60min
  • Spawn Rate Scaling: Starts at 1.6/s, increasing by +0.1 every 60 seconds.

The Triptych Map

The arena consists of three massive hexagonal sectors connected by high-energy portals.

Sector 0
ECONOMIC HEX
The cradle of resources. Features +15% Meteorite Drop Chance and +15% XP Gain. Best for early-game scaling.
Sector 1
COMBAT HEX
High-intensity conflict zone. Features +15% Enemy Spawn Rate and +15% Collision Damage. Optimal for kill-count farming.
Sector 2
DEFENCE HEX
The core of stability. Features +20% Max HP and +20% Health Regen. Ideal for surviving late-game waves.

Portal Logic

  • Manual Activation: Pay 5 Dust to open all portals in the current arena.
  • Short Duration: Portals stay open for only 10s.
  • Red Portal: Connects to Combat Hex.
  • Yellow Portal: Connects to Economic Hex.
  • Blue Portal: Connects to Defence Hex.

Teleportation involves a 3-second transfer delay where the camera pans to the destination.

Meteorite Fragments

Artifacts dropped from enemies that can be socketed into the inner Matrix ring to boost adjacent Hexes.

Quality Tiers

  • Broken: -2% Perk Value penalty.
  • Damaged: Baseline Performance.
  • New: +2% Perk Value bonus.

Boosts per neighboring meteorite or specific sector origin (Economic/Combat/Def).

Significant boosts for each connected Legendary Hex.
Resonance Pairs (Lvl 7-8)
Massive efficiency for connecting two compatible Legendary Hexes (e.g. Eco-Eco).

Matrix Efficiency

The Matrix consists of a center Chassis, 6 inner Diamond sockets, and 6 outer Hexagon sockets.

The Connection Rules

  • Chassis Resonance: The center slot is boosted by all 6 inner Diamonds.
  • Hex Multiplier: Each Legendary Hex is boosted by up to 4 adjacent Meteorites.
  • Efficiency Stacks: Meteorite perks add up. A 30% Efficiency meteorite makes a 10% Upgrade effectively 13%.
