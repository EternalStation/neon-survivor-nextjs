Patch Notes
VOID NEXUS - LIVE UPDATE LOG
1.0.1

Fixes

  • Portal: New visuals.
  • Music: Fixed music that was bugged on the online website.

Class Updates

Malware

  • Starting Range buff decreased from 300% to 150%.
  • Projectile speed buff per wall hit reduced from 20% to 5% to maintain stable projectiles at high level.

Aegis-Vortex

  • Increased chance to spawn projectile on 2nd orbit from 10% to 15%.
  • Increased chance to spawn projectile on 3rd orbit from 5% to 10%.
  • Added 4th orbit with 5% chance.

Elite Enemies

Elite Diamond

  • (Locked Phase) Increased from 0.6 to 0.8, giving you 0.8 seconds to move out of the line of fire.
  • Visual warning line added during the charge phase: starts as a thin, dim guide line while tracking.
  • Damage profile: confirmed and ensured Elite Diamond laser damage is reduced by armor.

Unique Enemies

Legion

  • Now spawns only one hexagon shape legionnaires (removed minion enemy type).
  • Total shield reduced from 200% to 100%.
  • Fixed bug where legionnaires could die while shield is active.
  • Legionnaires no longer die from walls.

Meteorites

  • Increased stable top chance to 7% (previously 2.5% to 1% from 0m to 60m).
  • Added corrupted type (3% drop chance): +6% basic range perk, but cannot be replaced.
  • Different drop chances by type: Broken 60%, Damaged 35%, Pristine 12% (previously 33% each).
  • Range bonus by type increased from -2% / 0% / +2% to -3% / 0% / +3% for a more visible difference.

Inventory Sorting

  • Added 10 safe slots protected from bulk recycle.

Module Matrix

  • Replacement price now grows over time instead of a flat 5 Dust.
  • Starts at 1 Dust and increases by +1 each minute.

Enemy HP Scaling

New Formula

Base HP: 60 (unchanged)

Exponential growth: 1.2 ^ minutes (was 1.186)

Cycle multiplier: 1.65 ^ 5-minute cycle (was 1.2)

Enemy Collision Damage

Old formula: 15% of enemy MAX HP

New formula: enemy MAX HP ^ 0.6

Example:

Old (15%): 50,000 HP * 0.15 = 7,500 Damage

New (Power 0.6): 50,000 ^ 0.6 = 660 Damage

Enemy Spawn Rate Scaling

Old

1.5/sec +0.1 every minute

New

  • 0-10 mins: +0.2 per minute (double the old rate)
  • 10-20 mins: +0.3 per minute (triple the old rate)
  • 20+ mins: +0.4 per minute (quadruple the old rate)

New

  • 8 new blueprints added.
  • Blueprints drop from Elite Merged enemies with 8% chance.
  • Decrypt them and spend Dust for strong buffs and life-saving options.

New Flankers Behavior

  • 10% chance for normal and elite enemies to spawn as flankers.

Flanking State (Status 0)

  • Orbits the player at 400-600px distance.
  • Chooses clockwise or counter-clockwise direction.
  • Rotates around player looking for an opening.

Striking State (Status 1)

  • Triggered when angle difference is greater than 126 degrees (pi * 0.7) and close to orbit position.
  • Charges directly at the player with 1.15x speed multiplier.
  • Strikes for 3-5 seconds before returning to flanking.

3 New Legendary Upgrades

  • Economic Arena: new legendary upgrade added.
  • Combat Arena: new legendary upgrade added.
  • Defence Arena: new legendary upgrade added.

Evacuation

  • Now you can collect 4,000 Dust and see what happens. Good luck.
